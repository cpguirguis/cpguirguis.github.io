About Me

Building digital experiences that matter

I'm a software engineer passionate about creating elegant solutions to complex problems. With a focus on modern web technologies and user-centered design, I build applications that are both beautiful and functional.

My approach is rooted in the belief that the best technology begins with a question, not an answer. I combine technical expertise with a deep curiosity for the people I'm building for, striving to create systems that are not only powerful and efficient but also intuitive, performant, and accessible to everyone. Every project is an opportunity to push boundaries and learn something new.

When I'm not at the keyboard, I'm frequenting Ontario's hiking and biking trails, diving into philosophy and sci-fi novels, and doing outreach through my local church. I relish a good chess match or a tennis rally anytime.
